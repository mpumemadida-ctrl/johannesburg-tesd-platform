# TESD Platform

Township Economic & Social Development Platform

Developed by AnaSport Analytics & Development (Pty) Ltd

Purpose:
To measure township socio-economic vitality using data analytics and evidence-based decision making.

Core Frameworks:
- TIPI
- URPI

Pilot City:
Johannesburg

# johannesburg-tesd-platform
Township Economic &amp; Social Development (TESD) Platform for measuring socio-economic vitality, urban regeneration, public safety, recreation access, and economic growth in Johannesburg townships through data analytics and impact scoring.
